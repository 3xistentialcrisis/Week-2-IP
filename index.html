About

This Application tells you your Akan name based on your birthday!

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture, and are given as day names which correspond to the day of the week that you were born!

Steps

  1. Enter your birthday through the form below
  2. Chose what gender you are
  3. Click the submit button to see what your Akan name is!
